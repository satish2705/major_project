Abstract

Machine Learning (ML) is revolutionizing drug discovery and personalized medicine by enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness. This paper investigates current ML applications, including drug candidate identification, drug interaction prediction, and disease mechanism analysis. A novel ML framework is introduced, integrating deep learning and reinforcement learning to improve drug efficacy prediction and treatment personalization. Experimental validation shows significant advancements in drug development speed, precision, and patient-specific therapy customization. The study underscores ML's potential to transform healthcare while addressing challenges such as data privacy and algorithmic bias.
